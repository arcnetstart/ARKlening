Benefits and yield of ARC

Masternodes are key nodes in the cryptocurrency network, responsible for its decentralization, high speed, verification and validation of transactions. Also, one of their main functions is to ensure the security and anonymity of transactions. For their work, masterminds receive the currency of the network in which they operate.

Masternodes can be wallets installed on a server or computer with a static IP address. Also, prerequisites for their operation include: – Round-the-clock activity on the network – A pledge transaction for a certain number of coins, which are blocked for the duration of the Masternode, and these coins cannot be spent.

Masternode is rewarded for its work in the network. Anyone can install an ARC Masternode and become part of our ecosystem! Masternode owners, just like Miners, are rewarded for maintaining and supporting the network. The reward per block is split equally between Masternode owners and Miners, allowing you to have two sources of passive income.
